Purple and Gold Scholarship

University of Washington

Selection for the Purple and Gold Scholarship is done by the Office of Admissions.  It is based predominately on academics, but also incorporates activities, achievements, leadership and socio-economic factors. The 2015-16 Purple and Gold Scholarships ranged from $3,000 to $7,500 per year.

Robotic Appreciation Scholarship

Tyler and Joyce Schilling

Children of FMC Technologies / Schilling Robotics employees are considered for this scholarship based on academics, community service and financial need.  Scholarships range from $5,000 to $10,000 per year
